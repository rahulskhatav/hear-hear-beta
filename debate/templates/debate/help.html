Help

Making the best use of Hear, Hear!

Have a suggestion? Want an improvement? Drop us an email and let us hear it!
How do I register?

  1. Select the option Register on the menu at the top.
  2. Enter an unique username that is your identity on the platform.
  3. Crunch in a few extra personal details.
  4. Choose a strong password, one you can remember!

Need help to login?

  1. You can view every debate, motion and point on Hear, Hear! But what fun is just reading?
  2. If you have already registered, select Login on the menu at the top. Enter your username and password and voila, you are in!
  3. If you haven't registered, what are you waiting for?
  4. Forgot to login? Don't worry, you'll be redirected to the login page when you try to login!

What's your Hear, Hear profile?

  1. To view your profile, after having logged in, select your username on the menu at the top. Voila, that's your profile!
  2. That's all the personal information we store.
  3. Today, a display picture speaks a thousand words! So dont be just a default! Update your profile picture today.
  4. Contributions help track your progress on Hear, Hear. Create a motion, write a blog, argue, rebut and earn contributions!

How to create a motion?

Have a great motion? Hear, Hear makes it extremely easy to host a debate!

  1. Go to Home via the menu on the top.
  2. Select Create Motion.
  3. Enter the motion title, a caption (optional) and select a suitable difficulty. Be as accurate as possible!
  4. Submit your motion. Voila, its up for a fun debate!

How to add arguments, points or a rebuttals?

Did a great motion grease the wheels in your head?

  1. Click the Hear, Hear! option of the motion of your choice.
  2. Scroll down to read existing arguments. Thought of something unheard? Make an argument!
  3. To explore an argument, clickExplore to read and make addtional points/rebuttals.
  4. Always segregate and make valid arguments with appropriate titles to have a good debate.

Hear, Hear also has a blog?!

Absolutely! Have an opinion? Want to share your invaluable knowledge? Blog It!

  1. To read and learn, select Blog on the menu at the top.
  2. Find a topic interesting? Click Hear, Hear! to read more on it.
  3. To write one, select Write a Blog on the Blog page.
  4. Choose a catchy title and caption and pen your thoughts away. Dont forget to Post.
